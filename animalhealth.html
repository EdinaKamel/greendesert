GreenDesert® Vital + EquiPlus

Reviving Soils. Greening Deserts. Securing the Future

GreenDesert® Vital and EquiPlus is a premium, all-natural feed additive developed to improve animal health, digestion, and productivity — from large-scale farms to elite equestrian centers. Based on active humic substances extracted from high-purity European leonardite, it offers a safe, chemical-free solution trusted across species and borders. Whether you're raising dairy cattle, breeding poultry, or training world-class competition and breeding horses,GreenDesert® Vital and EquiPlus delivers real, measurable benefits in performance, well-being, and long-term sustainability.
